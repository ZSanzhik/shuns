How to save money?

Create a budget and keep track of it:

  • Track your income and expenses regularly.
  • Create a budget that includes all your expenses and strive to stick to it.
  • This will help you understand where your money is going and where you can save.

Set aside part of your income:

  1. Put some portion of your income into savings on a regular basis.
  2. Set a savings goal and stick to it.
  3. This will create a financial cushion in case of unexpected expenses and invest in the future.

2 more advices:

Avoid excess:
Strive to make informed purchases. Before you make a purchase, consider whether it is really necessary. Avoid unnecessary spending on unnecessary things.
